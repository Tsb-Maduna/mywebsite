Portfolio Website

Goal:

The goal of this portfolio website is to showcase my skills, projects, and growth as a computer science student and a juniour software developer. It serves as a space where I document my learning journey, demonstrate my problem-solving approach, and present real-world projects I have worked on. Beyond highlighting technical abilities, this portfolio reflects my creativity, discipline, and passion for building meaningful digital solutions, while providing potential employers and collaborators with insight into how I think, learn, and create.

Progress:
Tuesday, 23 December 2025

At this stage, I have built a solid and professional portfolio website that clearly represents my skills and direction. The site features a responsive, well-structured layout, and clearly defined sections. I have also established a system for documenting project progress and learning milestones, allowing the portfolio to showcase not only completed work but also my problem-solving approach, consistency, and growth as a developer.

Next Step:
Tuesday, 23 December 2025

The next phase of my portfolio development focuses on optimization, perfomance, and visibility. I plan to improve the website's search engine ranking through effective SEO practices, perfomance tuning, and clean, accessible code. To enhance development efficiency and maintain consistency, I will leverage the use of CDNs. This approach allows me to focus on scalability, maintainability, and delivering a polished, professional user experience.

Reflection:
Tuesday, 23 December 2025

With this project being my first, I had a lot of realisations from the moment I started with it. The major thing I realised is that I should have started the project with this "documentation" page, as it would have allowed me to have a proper vision and enforce me to follow a smooth timeline with already set deadlines. From this experience I note the importance of documentation.

This project pushed me from a comfort zone and led me to self learning how to publish on Github, it introduced me to some basics of Javascript which opened my mind to a lot of possibilities that I intend to turn into real solutions. Most importantly, the success of this project motivates me to grow with an attitude that is bigger, smarter and better towards upcoming projects.
